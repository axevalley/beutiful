About Me

BE-u-tiful was started by me, Jade Reece, with the aim to provide personal, individual and effective ways to help with stress management, confidence, style and wellbeing.

After battling eating disorders, anxiety and depression I realised how important wellbeing and balance is along with having the tools to cope with those hard times that come to us all. That’s why I studied and achieved a diploma from the School of Natural Health Sciences in Stress Management, which I am also furthering with a Psychology & Counselling degree with the Open University.

Through personal experience I found that sometimes it’s the little things that can help us feel more “human”. Not everything works for everyone and just because it works for everyone and not you, it certainly doesn’t mean there is anything wrong with you! That’s why I specialised in bespoke and individual help to make sure people have the tools they need, when they need it.

Outside the BE-u-tiful world I am a keen archer, animal lover (I currently have a little Maximilian Pionus parrot called Scoops) and musician (saxophone and an attempt at the banjo). I was born in Somerset where I lived for 18 years before moving 200 miles away to Northamptonshire to increase my career prospects and have been bouncing around the county ever since. I love learning, reading, drawing, cooking and always manage to find time for a nap in between running BE-u-tiful, doing a degree and working full time.

Jade xo

About BE-U-tiful

BE-u-tiful is a personal service based individual well-being, stress management, style and skincare service. We specialise in tailoring to your needs - whether it be to gain a bit of confidence in yourself, find useful tools to cope with stress, increase your overall well-being or just help to make you feel better about yourself.

We have a range of different services from stress management consultations, home well-being and pamper parties through to individual client sessions on style and confidence. We can do local or online services and our specifically selected product ranges have been meticulously chosen to make sure we offer the best at the best prices.

We try to be as cruelty free and ethical as possible, which is why we don’t just look at the products but the company themselves. All companies are as ethical as possible, right down to the packaging, the employment of the people that make the products and what the products are made out of.

We aim to help you bring wellness and balance to your life in a way that works for you, on the inside and the outside.

Jade xo
